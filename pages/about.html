My Journey

I’m Andile Zikhali, a passionate full-stack developer, game creator, and AI enthusiast dedicated to building unique digital experiences. My expertise lies in developing interactive platforms, gaming solutions, and AI-driven applications that entertain, educate, and empower users. With experience at SEDA, Empire Partner Foundation, GameOn Africa, and Ushaka Gaming Studio, I strive to create impactful solutions.

My love for coding, problem-solving, and creativity drives me. I’m currently sharpening my skills through Fullstack Open, staying ahead in modern web and software development. I thrive on pushing boundaries and bringing ideas to life.
