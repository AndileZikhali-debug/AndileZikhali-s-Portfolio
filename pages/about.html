About Me

I am Andile Zikhali, a passionate full-stack developer, game creator, and AI enthusiast. I specialize in creating interactive platforms, gaming solutions, and AI-driven applications that entertain, educate, and empower users.

With experience working with SEDA, Empire Partner Foundation, GameOn Africa, and Ushaka Gaming Studio, I am committed to delivering unique and impactful digital solutions.

What I Do

Game Development & Aggregation

I created LELO Games, a game aggregator app for popular titles like FIFA, GTA, and Mortal Kombat. I'm also developing a school simulation game and a code-learning game.

AI & Cultural Preservation

Currently working on an AI-powered language learning platform to teach South African languages and preserve cultural heritage.

Web Development & Business Solutions

I develop platforms such as an accountant-business marketplace, MzansiVz (a Netflix-style streaming service), and my interactive portfolio site.
